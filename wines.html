2014 Bohemian Blend Red Wine

This wine drinks like a great Zin or Grenache. Great texture and weight in the mouth with substantial, yet soft, tannins. Just enough once-used oak barrels to lend a touch of vanilla and toast. It's a harmonious wine that should pair with all kinds of dishes from salmon to roasted red meat. Alcohol: 13.9% // 90 points WE Ageability: 5 to 10 years // Production: 98 cases

2014 Pinot Noir, Lester Family Vineyard, Santa Cruz Mtn

"Moderately light cherry red color in the glass. Lovely aromas of red cherry, baking spice, rose petal and sandalwood lead to an elegantly composed and delicious wine offering vivid red cherry and raspberry flavors and an appealing whole cluster spiciness. The dreamy texture adds to the pleasure as does the lip-smacking, cherry fueled finish. A seamless wine that offers loads of Pinot character." Score: 93 -Prince of Pinot

2013 Syrah, Flocchini Vineyard, Petaluma Gap

Production just 52 cases.

2016 À la Volée, Sparkling Riesling, Pét-nat

The eagerly anticipated new À la Volée Pétillant-naturel is crisp, fresh and terribly tasty. I skip the disgorging step and leave it on the lees for a fun and unique California sparkling wine. Grapes grown at Tondré Grapefield in the Santa Lucia Highlands. Production: just 12 cases produced.

2016 Rosé, Blair Vineyard, Arroyo Seco

Fermented in stainless steel but aged in barrels with weekly lees stirring. "Delicate apricot color in the glass. The nose is alive and sustained in the glass, offering aromas of apricot, strawberry, cherry, blood orange and a floral note. The delicious flavors of cherry, strawberry white peach and apricot have good weight and are bright and lifted. The wine is perfectly balanced with fresh acidity, finishing with a blood orange tang. The best domestic rosé that I have experienced in quite awhile." -Prince of Pinot: 93 Points.
