Welcome to Waxwing Wines

Our goal at Waxwing it to make the wines in a flight that you will want to keep drinking after you have pushed everything else away. We pride ourselves in our wines that show depth and integrity with bright acidity, compelling aromas and delicious flavors. To acheive this we source grapes from cool and windy vineyards up and down the California Coast. Our grapes are picked at modest brix levels, ferment with little or no additions, and age in broken-in barrels from a variety of French coopers.
